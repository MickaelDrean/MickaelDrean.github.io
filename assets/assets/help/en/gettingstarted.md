# Getting Started Guide

## **Managing Your Sessions**

### Create a New Session

**Simple Steps:**
1. Click on ![CreateSession](assets/help/en/images/SessionExplorationCreateSessionButton.png) **"New Session"**
2. Give your session a **name**
3. Choose the **date**
4. Add a **theme** (optional):
   - Your intention for this session
   - Theoretical elements to present

### Import a Session
If a colleague shares a session with you:
1. Click on ![LoadSession](assets/help/en/images/SessionExplorationLoadSessionButton.png) **"Load a Session"**
2. Select the received `.vab` file

### Actions on Your Sessions

## ![Edit](assets/help/en/images/SessionExplorationEditSessionButton.png) **Preparing a Session (Edit Mode)**

### Overview
Edit mode allows you to:
- ‚úÖ Add exercises and their music
- ‚úÖ Write your personal instructions
- ‚úÖ Organize the order of proposals
- ‚úÖ Modify title, date and theme

### Add a Proposal (exercise + music)

**Two options:**
- **Insert after** an existing proposal: ![AddAfter](assets/help/en/images/SessionEditionAddPropositionButton.png)
- **Add at the end**: ![AddEnd](assets/help/en/images/SessionEditionAddPropositionButton.png) (at the bottom of the screen)

### Modify Your Session Content

| What you want to do | How to proceed |
|--------------------------|------------------|
| **Change the title** | Click directly on the title (top right) |
| **Modify the date** | Click on the date (top right) |
| **Edit an instruction** | Click on the instruction text |
| **View a demonstration** | ![Video](assets/help/en/images/SessionEditionVideoButton.png) (if video available) |
| **Delete a proposal** | ![Delete](assets/help/en/images/SessionEditionDeletePropositionButton.png) |

### Useful Keyboard Shortcuts
- **Space**: Play/Pause music
- **Enter**: Play the music of the selected proposal
- **Arrow keys ‚Üë‚Üì**: Navigate between proposals
- **Shift + Arrow keys ‚Üë‚Üì**: Move a proposal
- **<**: Return to the beginning of the music and pause

## ![Edit](assets/help/en/images/SessionExplorationEditCurveSessionButton.png) **Editing on the Physiological Curve**

This editor allows you to position your proposals (exercise + music) on your session's curve.

### Possible Actions
- **Add**: Click on the curve
- **Modify**: Double-click on a proposal
- **Move**: Drag the colored point of the proposal on the curve
- **Mark as key proposal**: ![Key](assets/help/en/images/SessionEditionOnCurveKeyButton.png) (green frame)
- **Distribute automatically**: ![Redistribute](assets/help/en/images/SessionEditionOnCurveRedistributeButton.png)

## üîß **Practical Functions**

### Organize Your Sessions
- **Sort by**: Date, last modification, name, creation
- **Filter**: By period or presence of observations
- **Progress view**: ![HorizontalView](assets/help/en/images/SessionExplorationHorizontalSessionNavigationButton.png) to see evolution between sessions

### Advanced Search in Your Sessions
You can search in:
- Music titles and artists
- Session themes and titles
- Personal instructions and observations
- Exercise names

**Examples:**
- *artist:vangelis* ‚Üí all your sessions using Vangelis
- *theme:creativity* ‚Üí sessions about creativity
- *observations:resistance* ‚Üí where you noted resistance

### Save and Share
Click on ![Save or Share](assets/help/en/images/SessionSaveOrShareButton.png) to Save or share your session in one of the following formats:
- **Vivenciapp Format** (.vab): to send to a colleague who will load it into VivenciApp
- **Spreadsheet Format** (.csv): to keep your session in an open text format
- **Document Format** (.pdf): to have a printable version.

## üí° **Tips to Get Started**

### For Your First Session
1. **Create** a new session with a simple name
2. **Explore** the catalog to familiarize yourself
3. **Add** 2-3 simple proposals
4. **Test** animation mode without participants

### Best Practices
- ‚úÖ Name your sessions clearly (e.g., "Identity - Beginner Group")
- ‚úÖ Use themes to note your intentions
- ‚úÖ Note your observations after each session
